Eatsy ("we," "us," "our") is a nutrition and food-tracking app. This policy explains what information we collect when you use Eatsy, why we collect it, who we share it with, and the choices you have — including how to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

01 Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information directly from you:

  • Account information: your name and email address, used to create and secure your account.
  • Body and health metrics: weight, height, age, gender, and activity level, used to calculate calorie and macro targets.
  • Dietary preferences: cuisine preferences, diet type (e.g. vegetarian, keto), allergies, and any medical conditions you choose to share (e.g. diabetes, hypertension) — used solely to personalize your AI-generated meal plans and exclude foods you can't or don't want to eat.
  • Food and nutrition logs: foods you log, including calories and macros, meal type, and timestamps.
  • Water intake logs.
  • Goals: your calorie, macro, and weight targets.
  • Barcode data: if you use the Barcode Scan feature, the scanned barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts (see Section 3) to look up nutrition information. No photo is stored.

The camera-based food-photo recognition feature is not yet active in the current version of Eatsy — no photo data is transmitted or stored by that feature today. This policy will be updated before that feature is enabled.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, or browsing history from other apps.

02 How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate your personalized calorie and macro targets.
  • To generate AI-personalized meal plans (see "Third-Party Services" below — this involves sending your goals and food preferences, but not your name or email, to our meal-planning AI provider).
  • To show you your nutrition history, trends, and progress over time.
  • To operate core account functions: authentication, password reset, and account deletion.
  • We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

03 Third-Party Services

Eatsy relies on the following third parties to operate. Each only receives the specific data needed for its function:

  • Supabase — our backend database and authentication provider. All the data described above is stored on Supabase's infrastructure.
  • Groq — an AI provider used to generate meal plans and meal swaps. Your calorie/macro goals, recent food preferences, and dietary restrictions are sent to Groq to generate a plan; your name, email, and identity are not included in that request.
  • Open Food Facts — a public, open food database used to look up nutrition information when you search for or scan a food item. Search terms and barcodes are sent to Open Food Facts; no account information is shared with them.
  • Google Sign-In, if you choose to sign in with Google, is used only to authenticate you — governed by Google's own privacy policy for that specific interaction.

04 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account through the app (Profile → Delete Account), all of the data categories listed in Section 1 are permanently deleted from our systems immediately, along with the account itself. This action cannot be undone.

05 Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and correction: you can view and edit most of your data directly within the app (Goals, Diet Preferences, Food Log).
  • Deletion: you can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Profile → Delete Account, with no need to contact us.
  • Questions or requests outside of what the app supports directly can be sent to the contact below.

06 Data Security

Data is stored with Supabase and protected by row-level security policies restricting each account to its own data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

07 Children's Privacy

Eatsy is not directed at children and is not intended for use by anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

08 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app changes. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do. Continued use of Eatsy after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
